About App

This app was born out of a need to amalgamate the processes involved in keeping my car maintained - which involved many different spreadsheets and procedures - and present them in a format that allowed me to view and carry them out anywhere.

The focus of this app is to keep the interface responsive and visually appealing, so that as my devices are upgraded the interface remains useable. This also serves as a demonstration of my understanding of designing apps.
